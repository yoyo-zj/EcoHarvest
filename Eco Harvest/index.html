Our Solution

GYRO

Growth Yield Revolutionize Optimization

- Specifically designed to optimize the rate of photosynthesis through light diffusion and increase growth rates by 235%.

- A revolutionizing biotech, that will lead to higher crop yields, global food security, and much more.

- Universally works on all genomes of plants. Implementations on a plethora of plants (e.g tobbaco, grain, potatoes, corn and etc)

- Exceptionally efficient, every component of GYRO, is utilized during the photosynthesis process. Creating zero waste.

Our Product

A revolutionizing spray made to Specifically target the receptors on a plant's cell wall or membrane to increase their size with synthetic cytokinins. To allow more light and nutrients to be absorded by the plant, increasing the photosynthesis rate through higher light diffusion rates. Simultaneously, still doing the job of a fertilizer. Certainly, our product is a breakthrough for the agricultural industry.
